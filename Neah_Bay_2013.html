Neah Bay, August 2013
Some image highlights from our wonderful Neah Bay trip the first week of August.  Due to my health, I only made 5 dives - all of them fabulous and easy.  I was truly grateful to get to see and dive with some old friends - some of the scuba variety and others of the finned variety.  I have been noting two rosy rockfish at Third Beach Pinnacle for about ten years now, although I have not seen them the prior two years.  I was very please to run into one of the rosies this year, and as usual it posed for a few pics.  Weather was superb, but vis underwater was subpar ranging from 15 to 30 feet.  Health permitting, Jon and I will be back again next summer.  Thank you again, Neah Bay.   You continue to inspire as we are truly blessed by nature in this part of the world.
