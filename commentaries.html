This page serves as my "soap box" where I express my opinions on certain matters relating to diving and the environment.  My opinion regarding the environment (and even diving, for that matter) are not always the most popular.  The original intent of this website was to educate people regarding the wonders of Pacific Northwest waters.  Although there is still unimaginable beauty in our local waters, there is also a lot of ugliness which is the direct result of human pressures.  Only by understanding the bad and the good can we make the Pacific Northwest a better place for all creatures.

If you take offense to some of my stands on solo diving, toxins in Puget Sound, orcas, or spearfishing, feel free to start your own website.  Enter at you own risk, and please do so with an open mind.
